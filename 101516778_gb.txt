George Brown College (GBC) is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 
It offers a wide variety of diploma, degree, and certificate programs in areas such 
as technology, business, health sciences, and community services. 


GBC is known for its strong industry connections and emphasis on hands-on, practical 
learning. The college also provides opportunities for applied research, co-op programs, 
and partnerships with leading employers in Canada. 

With a diverse student body and multiple campuses across Toronto, George Brown College 
prepares students with the skills and experience needed to succeed in their careers.
